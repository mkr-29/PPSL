Read a sequence of input integers until -1 is read. Output sum of numbers read, not including the negative number.
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Input Format:
1
4
5
6
-1
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Constraints:
integer numbers as input
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Output Format:
16
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Sample Input 0:
2
4
6
-1
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Sample Output 0:
12
