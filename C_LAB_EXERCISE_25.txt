INPUT A POSITIVE INTEGER N.
TAKE THE RECIPROCAL SUM OF ALL POSITIVE INTEGERS FROM 1 UPTO N.
PRINT THE SUM.
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
eg.:
INPUT N =4
(1/1) + (1/2) + (1/3) + (1/4)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
OUTPUT RECRIPOCRAL SUM UPTO 2 PLACES OF DECIMALS.
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Input Format:
2
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Constraints:
ENTER POSITIVE INTEGER LESS THAN 100 FLOAT SUM PRINTED UPTO 2 PLACES OF DECIMAL
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Output Format:
1.50
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Sample Input 0:
3
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Sample Output 0:
1.83
